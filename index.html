Our Story

Untouched backwaters,
authentic experiences

Honnavar is one of Karnataka's best-kept secrets — a pristine coastal town where the Sharavathi river meets the Arabian Sea. We've been guiding travellers through these enchanting backwaters for over a decade.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful sunrise boat ride, an adventurous fishing expedition, or a comfortable stay in our river-facing rooms — we promise experiences that are genuine, safe, and unforgettable.
